This is Sara, happy to see you around :)

So if you are a gardening enthusiast, I hope my page has been of some use to you. I have tried to refer to as many verified data sources and am still building up on it. So if I haven't covered your region yet then don't worry, I will try to reach there asap, meanwhile do read some of the blog posts related to gardening or environmental sustainability in general!


I have always been an explorer and I love nature at it's purest. During lockdown last year, one of the outlets for all this energy to do something useful and good for the environment ended up with gardening. I've been lucky with an agri-scientist superDad throughout my garden journey but for those who majorly depend on the internet for info about the climate around their region or which plants to sow when, I thought I could help in some way :)


A little research into the importance of local cultivation showed the extent of positive impact it can have on the environment, in terms of reduced Carbon emissions and single use plastics. I believe locally produced and marketed food, especially crops can be profitable and better for the environment. Just requires some conscious consumption from those who can do so, for the environment and for their own health. Meanwhile, disruptive innovation in Agri-Tech is definitely underway!
